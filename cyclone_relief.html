Amphan Cyclone Relief

 

Cyclone Amphan, one of the worst storms over the Bay of Bengal in years, has left a trail of destruction in Bengal after making landfall on the eve of May 20th, 2020.
The loss of resources, agriculture and infrastructure has been unprecedented as the largest super cyclone since 1997 made landfall in the Indian State.
