About The Program

  

Google collaborates with university students who are passionate about growing developer communities.

Developer Student Club, is a university based community group that intends to help you bridge the gap between theory and practice. By joining DSC PGDAV, you can grow your knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and find a way to turn all your ideas into reality!

Learn more about the Program

 

 

What DSC PGDAV do?

 

Developer Student Club PGDAV is inspired by the Google Developers' Family.

The motive is to create a local ecosystem of Developers in and around the Campus. And having fun doing it.
